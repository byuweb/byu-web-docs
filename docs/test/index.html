BYU Websites

Simple, free web hosting.

BYU Websites is a new service to improve and simplify campus web publishing. It will provide cloud hosting, security, round-the-clock monitoring, and accessible design templates.

The first sites will launch during Spring term 2019.

Move your site

To have your site moved to BYU Websites, contact the BYU Websites Manager, Nate Walton (sites@byu.edu).
